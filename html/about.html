About Us

Melody Creators was created by Jessie Peng with the illustrations done by Michelle Peng.

In August 2018, Jessie interviewed and got the 'Classroom Assistant' position at Regent Park School of Music. Although originally, her motives were to attain volunteer hours this opportunity soon grew beyond that. From weekly sessions with teachers and students of all ages, she realized that it was not only her students' music abilities growing; her own passion for music and confidence in herself grew as well. Continue to volunteer since then, she will be finishing her 2nd year as a 'Classroom Assistant'.

She hopes this website will be a resource for fellow musicians looking to rekindle their love for music, beyond endless practising and performing, as well as people who want to take advantage of their musical abilities to give back to the community!
